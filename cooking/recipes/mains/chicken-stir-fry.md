## Ingredients

- 500g chicken breast, sliced thin
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- Green onions for garnish

## Instructions

1. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch in a small bowl.
2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
3. Add chicken and stir-fry until cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
4. Add remaining oil, then stir-fry vegetables for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp.
5. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 30 seconds.
6. Return chicken to pan, add sauce mixture.
7. Toss everything together until sauce thickens, about 1 minute.
8. Garnish with green onions and serve over rice.

## Notes

- Prep all ingredients before you start cooking
- High heat is key for a good stir fry
- Feel free to use any vegetables you have on hand
- Add chili flakes for a spicy version
